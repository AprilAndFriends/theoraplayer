This wrapper is used in the "April & Friends" project developed by the author of Theora Playback Library.

To compile and use this, you'll need:
* gtypes
* hltypes
* april
* atres
* aprilui
* ogg
* vorbis
* theora
* theoraplayer

If you have any questions, feel free to use Theora Playback Library's forum
or contact me directly through Sourceforge.
